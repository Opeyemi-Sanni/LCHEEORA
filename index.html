I present to you MR. DIRAN FAMAKINWA, an esteemed Resident of LCH who has been jointly and severally nominated by Messrs Ademola Adedeji, Femi Oladele, Sanmi Abejide and Mercy Taiwo as “CHAIRMAN” of the LCHEEROA as advertised by the 2024 electoral committee.

Mr. Oladiran Julius Famakinwa FCCA FCA MBA is currently the Senior Partner of Jorshwel Consulting Limited, a Financial Consultancy Firm. Prior to that, he served as the Chief Financial Officer of St. Nicholas Hospital Lagos from March 2013 to September 2021. He also worked in various organizations in the United Kingdom, rising to the position of Finance Director of Homerton Hospital, London. Mr. Famakinwa is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) and the Chartered Association of Certified Accountants (ACCA). To further expand his business knowledge, he attended the London Southbank Business School and was awarded a Master's in Business Administration (MBA)

Mr. Famakinwa has served on several professional committees, including the followings: ACCA Public Sector Technical Panel. Chairman of the UK ICAN District in 2006/07. In 2008, he received the Gathering of Africa Best (GAB) Award in the Finance Category from Trumpet Newspaper, UK. The ICAN UK District also honored him with a Golden Medal award for his excellent services to the UK District.

Deeply involved in charity and social activities, Mr. Famakinwa is the current Chairman & Trustee of IA Foundation Ltd, a UK-based charity dedicated to rescuing "Out of School Children" in Nigeria. The charity currently sponsors over 120 pupils in various educational institutions.

In addition to his professional accomplishments, Mr. Famakinwa holds several leadership positions in various organizations. He is the current Chairman of the Ikoyi Club 1938, Tennis Section, and the Global President of the Fellowship of 1980s Leos (associated with Lions Club International). He is also a member of the 80s Rotalum (Alumni of Rotaract Club).

Aside from his professional and philanthropic engagements, Mr. Famakinwa is a musician and the Bass Guitarist at the First Baptist Church, Festac.

I present to you MAJ. GEN ADEKUNLE SHODUNKE (Rtd.) an esteemed Resident of LCH who has been nominated as “EX OFFICIO 2” of the LCHEEROA as advertised by the 2024 electoral committee.

Maj Gen Adekunle SHODUNKE (Rtd.) is an experienced and well travelled military veteran who recently retired from the Nigerian Army. His meritorious military Service spanned over 38 years, where he rose through the ranks and got to the peak of his career as a Major General in the Nigerian Army.

Gen Shodunke's career took him to many military operations, both home and abroad. He held different posts and appointments at all levels while in Service. At various times during his career, he was a staff officer, instructor, Directing Staff in Armed Forces Command and Staff College, Commanding Officer, Brigade commander, Director of Works at Headquarters Nigerian Army Engineers, Commandant of Nigerian Army School of Military Engineering (NASME) in Makurdi, the Chief of Army Standards and Evaluation at the Army Headquarters as well as Chief of Defence Logistics at the Defence Headquarters Abuja before he retired.

He is also a well-decorated veteran with several honours, awards and medals to his credit while in Service. Since he retired from active Service, he has engaged in several vocations. These include philanthropic activities, Agricuture, Real Estate, Security Consultancy and training as well as Community Development. He was trained as a Certified Protection Officer (CPO) at the International Foundation for Protection Officers (IFPO) based in Tulsa, USA and went through the Senior Executive Managers program as well as Management Consultant training at the Administrative Staff College of Nigeria (ASCON).

He is a Fellow of many organisations and institutions, which include;
the Nigerian Army Resource Centre (FNARC),
Nigerian Institute of International Affairs (FNIIA),
Fellow of Nigerian Institute of Industrial Security (FNIIS),
Fellow of international institute of Professional Security (FIIPS),
Fellow of Institute For Tourism Professionals of Nigeria (FITPN) and
Fellow International Association of Strategy Professionals (FIASP) Nigeria Affiliate.

Gen Shodunke (Rtd) is married with children. He loves inspirational messages, nature, adventure watching nature photography.
